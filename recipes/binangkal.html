Binangkal

Binangkal is a recipe that is native to Visayas and Mindanao. Unlike most donuts, binangkal is mostly chewy paired with sesame seeds and crispy crust which is a perfect snack for kids.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons salted butter melted
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup evaporated milk
  • 1/2 cup sesame seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla optional

Steps

  1. Combine all dry ingredients in a bowl: flour, cornstarch, sugar, salt, and baking powder. Mix well
  2. In a separate bowl mix together milk,melted butter, and egg. Mix well.
  3. Slowly add the dry ingredients into the wet mixture. Mix well to form a dough.
  4. Scoop a tablespoon mixture to form a ball and roll it into the sesame seeds.
  5. Prepare oil in a pan for deep frying. Make sure the oil is hot before putting the dough.
  6. Deep fry unntil golden brown.
